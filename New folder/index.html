About Us

Overview

Nasha Nivaran Board has been constituted to tackle the drug menace under the chairmanship of Chief Minister Jai Ram Thakur. Nasha Nivaran Board will coordinate between various agencies of the state and suggest ways and means to tackle the menace of inter-state drug trafficking, production and distribution within the state.

Nasha Nivaran Board, a substantially dependable organisation which can undoubtedly deliver the good and as such cope up drug menace and can ensure the uninterrupted flow of information to the beneficiaries of the State. The board continues to perform from behind the curtain without the least intention to come on the forefront just to make others see the services it renders.
